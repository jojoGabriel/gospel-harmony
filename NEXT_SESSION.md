## Established Working Standard

The manuscript is no longer aiming for loose paraphrase or commentary-style summary.

The agreed standard is:

- stay close to what Scripture is saying
- avoid copying the distinctive wording of a copyrighted English translation
- prefer expanded harmony in major speeches, testimony scenes, prophecies, and discourses
- preserve direct speech where the biblical text is speech-heavy
- avoid third-person report style when it makes the material sound like summary
- keep paragraph-level references for review

In practice, this means the text should sound like a close retelling of the Gospel material, not like an explanation of it.
